Revolutionizing Battery Health: AI-Powered DTT

MOSart Labs' proprietary Digital Twin Tracking (DTT) technology is at the heart of the ML1024. It uses high-precision, real-time IVT data and AI/ML algorithms to create a 'digital twin' of each battery cell. This enables predictive monitoring and optimized balancing, setting a new industry benchmark.
